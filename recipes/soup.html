Soup

Homemade soup that can be paired with rice as well. Well cooked meal where you can enjoy a nice filling meal on a cold day.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Carrot
  • Noodles
  • pre-cooked meat small pieces
  • garlic
  • water
  • salt

Steps

  1. Simmer water in the pot and cook the meat you will be using for the soup.
  2. Add the ingredients one by one and always simmer every couple of minutes.
  3. Don't add too much salt and don't add the noodles to quick to prevent it from being overcooked
  4. Should be done in the next 30-40 minutes
